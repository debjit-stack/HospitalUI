About Unity Health Group

Founded in 1995, Unity Health has grown from a small community clinic to a leading multi-specialty hospital in the region. Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare to all.

We believe in a holistic approach to medicine, combining advanced medical treatments with compassionate, patient-focused care. Our dedicated staff works tirelessly to create a healing environment where patients feel safe, respected, and well-cared for.
